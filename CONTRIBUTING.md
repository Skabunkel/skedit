# Contributing

AI is welcome but in order to contribute to this repository you must firstly be a practitioner of digital
thaumaturgy, and secondly you have to swear the ancient contributor's [oath](#the-oath).
Finally you should be ready to uphold everything Unseen University (UU) stands for.

## The Oath

I, **[contributor's name]**, do solemnly swear by **[contributor's framework of choice]** to
uphold the Code Style and Conventions of the repository of **[project name]**, serve the
public API, and defend the users of His/Her/Their *(delete whichever is inappropriate)*
Maintainer **[name of reigning maintainer]** without fear, mass refactoring, or thought of
personal productivity; to pursue bugs and protect the innocent codebase, laying down my
weekend if necessary in the cause of said duty, so help me **[aforesaid framework]**.

**[contributor's Deity/Deities of choice, or lack of such, whatever it may be]** save the
CI pipeline *(delete if already broken)*.

---

## In practice, this means

Please open an issue before submitting large changes. Keep commits atomic, tests passing,
and clippy happy. Be kind in reviews we are all Wizards here.
